Voice capture

Tap, talk, walk away. Two states — idle (big mic, call-to-action) and recording (live waveform, timer, stop). Tap the red square to commit; tap Discard to throw it away. Works offline; transcription happens server-side once you hit signal.

⚠ Sample data only.
Site context "14 Sycamore Ln" is a placeholder. At runtime this flows from whichever site the user is currently inside when they hit Record.

Tap to record

Big orange mic, stamp shadow, dashed ring as a tap target. Site context chip up top so you never lose track of which site the memo belongs to.

02 · Recording

Live waveform

Red dot pulses, timer counts up to a 3-minute cap. Bars pulse for the last 12 (the "live" tail). Bottom: Discard · Stop · Pause. Red square = stop and save.
